Andaman and Tourism Industry

Tourism is a major industry in Andaman.The bulk of the revenue earned by the government of Andaman and Nicobar is through the tourism industry.

In 2008 total 136,426 tourists visited Andaman and Nicobar. Growing sectors in tourism and potential area of investment are water sports and adventure tourism including trekking, island camping, snorkeling and scuba diving. Sea aquarium, water theme park, wave surfing, marina yacht, convention centre, health resorts, sanctuaries, national park, inter-island cruise liner.
